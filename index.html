About

Human State-Aware Robotics: From Multimodal Data to Human-Adaptive Behavior in HRI.

This workshop focuses on connecting multimodal human data to adaptive robot behavior—methods, datasets, studies, systems, and lessons learned— with an emphasis on robustness, responsible data practices, and deployment in the wild.

Call for Participation
